Features Implemented:

  • Visual Badge: Out-of-stock products display a gray "Out of Stock" badge next to the product name
  • Sorting: Out-of-stock items appear at the bottom of search results (Products 2 and 4 are out of stock)
  • Filter Option: Checkbox to hide out-of-stock items from the display
  • Disabled Button: Out-of-stock products have a disabled "Unavailable" button instead of "Add to Cart"
